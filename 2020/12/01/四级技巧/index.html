Posted on 2020-12-01

四级必过

### 常见的土单词排行榜 1. **think** - have been convinced that - be of the opinion that

  1. many

    • a sea of
    • multitudes of
    • immense amounts of

  2. people we us

    • youngsters and teenagers
    • all children and adults
    • experts and professors

  3. very

    - more than 
- outstandingly

  4. important

    - play a crucial role in sth.
- significant

  5. And

        - in the meanwhile
    - at the same time

写作中可用的万能句型：

  • It has been found that

定语从句

  • He is a lady killer, which has provoked the public’s widespread concern.

万能状语

  1. as every one can see it
  2. with the rapid advance of science and technology
  3. in our contemporary society
  4. in the general routine of everyday living

插入语

  1. as a matter of fact
  2. to tell the truth

强调句型

  • It is … that…

  • It is the yesterday that I met a crazy dog in the street

写作与翻译

谚语警句类

第一段引出主题+ 解释你对这句话的理解

  • Nowadays, there remains an increasing interest in the topic about…;

第二、三句：解释这句话的意思

  • the meaning of the saying seems that …

第三段总结段

  • It is really high time that due attention cannot have failed to paid to the issue

第二、三句话具体措施

  • Awareness about sth could be cultivated to make ourselves lead a healthy and favorable life.

最后一句： 喊口号！

  • Only by taking these action can people have a more brilliant and glorious future.
书信作文

文中未给出： 1. dear sir or madam

第一段 自我介绍+写作目的

  • 自我介绍： I am senior from the department of __ in__ university.

  • 写作目的： I am, to be frank, writing the letter in order to (文中一定给出）

第二段 按文中要求来写；

  • 比如推荐旅游景点: There, to begin with, are so many beautiful flowers and trees on campus. You, undoubtedly, will enjoy it.

第三段

  • 表示感谢： A. My thanks to you for your generous assistance are beyond words.

  • 期待回信： A I am looking forward to your reply.
